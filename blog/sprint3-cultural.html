Emotional Intelligence

23 November 2020 | S3-Core

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is a few things. It is about awareness of not just your own emotions or feelings, but also others. It is also about understanding your own feelings you are aware of and knowing what triggers them etc. And finally having tools and strategies to get the best out of your emotions. Those can include healthy habits.

How is it different to IQ?

I feel like IQ and EQ use different parts of the brain, and require a different type of thinking. IQ is about solving problems technically, whereas EQ is tied directly to emotions, and honestly feels like a ‘sixth sense’. Being able to read yourself & others.

Why is it important?

It is important because we are all humans with different qualities & faults, strengths and weaknesses. But we also have no choice but to navigate the world, which obviously means interacting with other humans. It affects all aspects of our lives, Family, workplace & out and about.
